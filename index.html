How it works

01. Money Disbursed

Everydaymoney offers a unique feature that allows users to set aside a portion of their income to be disbursed back to them on a daily, weekly, or monthly basis.

Our goal is to assist individuals in developing a saving culture by utilizing the internet, mobile technology, and our intuitive budgeting tool to produce the next generation of wealth in Africa.

We aim to motivate and empower users to make informed decisions about their money. We want to help users build a brighter financial future and achieve financial stability and security.
